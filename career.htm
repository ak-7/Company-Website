OVERVIEW

Hercules is a team of more than 50000 professionals spread across the globe. We combine a proven track record and professional skills, woven together with a culture of trust & caring. Our approach is built on empowering employees to take on challenging assignments and explore their potential.
We love what we do With over 50% of its workforce below the age of 30, Hercules is a young company that is over 75 years old. Every year we induct large batches of fresh engineering graduates. At every step of our employees' careers, we invest in them by offering learning avenues, thus expanding their skill sets to serve clients need better.
Diversity and Equal Opportunity We welcome diversity at the workplace. Our efforts are therefore focused on sustaining and enhancing this diverse work environment. It introduces fresh perspectives and enables our people to integrate better in society. We are an equal opportunity employer and adhere to statutory & regulatory norms.
